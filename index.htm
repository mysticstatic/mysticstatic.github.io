About Us

Mystic Static HQ

Our Story

Mystic Static is an independent game studio based out of Charlotte, North Carolina. It was founded in February 2013 by Dmitry Pustovit and Kelley Feng.

Mystic Static was founded with a simple goal, make games that we wanted to play and tell stories that we wanted to tell through video games. Everything we do is to stay true to this very goal.

The Games We Make

We put our hearts and souls into every game that we make, not to mention a lot of long nights and tiring days.

The characters we create, the environments we design, and the stories we tell are through what we believe is an amazing experience for the player.

Every game we make is a brilliant example of just how dedicated we are. Although we are a small studio we try our hardest to produce the best content that we can.

Our Current Projects

Mystic Static is currently working hard to release its first game, Quantum Jump. Support our progress on KickStater.
